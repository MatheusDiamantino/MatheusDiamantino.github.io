Wolfbit Postmortem

January 18, 2017

Wolfbit was a game done by me and two other friends for the Ludum Dare 35, where I was responsible for the art, oddly enough. It was a different experience for me, as I never had done any pixel art before in my life and I had to do it in three days in a sort of decent way.
